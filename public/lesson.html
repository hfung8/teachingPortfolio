Sample Lesson

This lesson went well and having three different levels for the students to learn from helped with differentiating the work. However, a lot of the students later told me that the level of code was too high for them to comprehend. I believe that it could have been better if we introduced it to them in separate chunks, as opposed to giving them all the code in one lesson.
In anticipation for the performance task, the lesson was important to help students get acquainted with what they would eventually need to hand in. Overall, although there were some setbacks with their comprehension of the code, I think that students were better prepared for the performance task because they had an experience that they could compare to from beforehand.
